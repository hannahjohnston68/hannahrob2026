Our Story

Our journey began seven years ago, on September 12th, 2018, at Sneaky Dee's – Rob's pick for killer nachos. Over that first plate, our shared obsession with dogs sparked instant connection and lots of laughs. Since then, life has been an adventure, from Isle of Wight road trips to exploring Mexican temples. Every moment has deepened our love and laughter.

After three years, we moved in to an apartment at Yonge & Eglinton, a nostalgic neighbourhood for Rob. Revisiting old haunts like the comedy club and brunch spot, along with peaceful Sherwood Park strolls, has made it truly ours. These years saw Hannah earn her Master's and move into software, while Rob followed his dream of working in veterinary medicine. On our five-year anniversary, Rob's surprise proposal at Alexander Muir Memorial Garden, where we first became official, was unforgettable, especially with our dog and friends there. Two months later, our mischievous puppy Boji arrived, bringing endless joy (and a bit of chaos!) We cherish quiet weekends with classic comedies, Survivor, games of tennis (friendly or fierce!), cooking, and dog walks, so grateful for all our shared moments, big and small.
